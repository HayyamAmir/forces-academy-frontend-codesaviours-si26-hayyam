Admissions

Join Forces Academy Faisalabad and begin your journey toward academic excellence, discipline, and a brighter future.

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to complete your admission process.

  1. Visit the academy or contact our admission office for guidance.
  2. Choose the program or course you want to apply for.
  3. Fill out the admission form with correct personal and academic details.
  4. Submit the required documents along with the form.
  5. Pay the admission/registration fee as instructed by the academy.
  6. Receive confirmation of your admission and class schedule.

Eligibility Criteria

Check who can apply for each program offered by Forces Academy.

FSc Pre-Medical

Students who have completed Matric with Science and want to continue in medical-related studies.

FSc Pre-Engineering

Students who have completed Matric with Science and wish to pursue engineering and technical fields.

Matric Science

Students who have completed middle school and want to continue their education in the science group.

Matric Arts

Students who have completed middle school and want to continue their education in the arts group.

Entry Test Preparation

Students who are currently studying or have completed intermediate and want to prepare for entry tests.

ICS

Students who have completed Matric and are interested in Computer Science, Mathematics, and technology.

Required Documents

Please keep the following documents ready at the time of admission.

  • Copy of B-Form / CNIC
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Previous academic result card / certificate
  • School leaving certificate (if applicable)
  • Parent / Guardian CNIC copy
  • Admission form with complete details
